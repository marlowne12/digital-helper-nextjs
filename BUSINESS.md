## Brand Voice & Tone

### Do
- Be direct, not salesy
- Show technical credibility without jargon
- Focus on results and ROI
- Mention Tri-Cities / local connection
- Use short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)

### Don't
- Use "cutting-edge", "revolutionary" (overused)
- Say "synergy", "leverage" constantly
- Write long walls of text
- Use generic agency speak
- Overpromise or hype

### Key Messages
1. "We build systems that work while you sleep"
2. "Pay only for results" (pay-per-lead)
3. "Modern tech, not WordPress templates"
4. "Local agency, not outsourced"

### Words to Use
- Transform, modernize, automate
- Qualified, verified, targeted
- Fast, instant, 24/7
- Local, Tri-Cities, community
- Results, ROI, growth

### Words to Avoid
- "Cutting-edge" (overused)
- "Revolutionary" (overpromising)
- "Synergy" (corporate cringe)
- "Utilize" (just say "use")
- "Leverage" in every sentence

## Service Page Template Structure

Each service page should include:

1. **Hero Section**
   - Problem/solution headline
   - Supporting subheadline
   - Primary CTA + secondary CTA

2. **Pain Points**
   - 3-5 specific problems your target audience faces
   - Use quotes from actual customer conversations
   - Agitate the problem briefly

3. **Solution**
   - How you fix it
   - What makes your approach different
   - Why it works

4. **Process**
   - 3-4 step approach
   - Clear, simple language
   - Visual timeline if possible

5. **Deliverables**
   - Specific, tangible items they receive
   - No vague promises
   - Checklist format works well

6. **Results/Proof**
   - Stats, testimonials, case study links
   - Before/after comparisons
   - Specific numbers (not "increased traffic" but "156% increase")

7. **Pricing Preview**
   - Starting at / packages available
   - Link to full pricing page
   - Transparent, not hidden

8. **FAQ Section**
   - 4-6 common questions
   - Address objections
   - Build trust

9. **Final CTA**
   - Book a call or get quote
   - Low-friction offer (free audit, consultation)
   - Multiple contact options
