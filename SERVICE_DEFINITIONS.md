## Content Writing Guidelines

### Voice & Tone
- Direct, not salesy
- Technical credibility without jargon
- Friendly but professional
- Results-focused
- Local pride (Tri-Cities references)

### Words to Use
- Transform, modernize, automate
- Qualified, verified, targeted
- Fast, instant, 24/7
- Local, Tri-Cities, community
- Results, ROI, growth

### Words to Avoid
- "Cutting-edge" (overused)
- "Revolutionary" (overpromising)
- "Synergy" (corporate cringe)
- "Utilize" (just say "use")
- "Leverage" in every sentence

### Formatting Rules
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Use bullet points sparingly
- Bold key phrases for scanners
- One CTA per section
- Headers should be skimmable
